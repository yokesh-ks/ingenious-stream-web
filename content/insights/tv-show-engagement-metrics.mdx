Track viewer retention, binge-watching patterns, and completion rates for popular TV series.

## Completion Rates by Genre

- **Crime Dramas:** 78% completion rate
- **Sci-Fi Series:** 82% completion rate
- **Reality TV:** 45% completion rate
- **Comedy Shows:** 65% completion rate
- **Documentary Series:** 70% completion rate

## Binge-Watching Patterns

**Average binge session:** 3.2 episodes

### Peak Viewing Times
- **Weekdays:** 8-10 PM (45% of views)
- **Weekends:** 2-4 PM (35% of views)
- **Late Night:** 10 PM-2 AM (20% of views)

## Viewer Retention Analysis

### Episode Drop-off Rates
- **Episode 1:** 100% (starting viewers)
- **Episode 2:** 92% retention
- **Episode 3:** 88% retention
- **Episode 5:** 78% retention
- **Episode 10:** 65% retention

### Key Findings
- Sci-fi shows have highest completion rates
- Reality TV shows lowest retention after episode 3
- Crime dramas maintain steady viewership throughout seasons
