Deep dive into genre preferences across different demographics and regions.

## Key Insights

**Overall Market Share:**
- **Action:** 35% of total views
- **Drama:** 28% of total views
- **Comedy:** 22% of total views
- **Horror:** 8% of total views
- **Romance:** 7% of total views

## Demographic Breakdown

### Age Groups
- **18-24:** Action movies preferred by 2:1 ratio over other genres
- **25-34:** Drama and comedy equally popular
- **35-44:** Family-friendly content shows 40% increase
- **45+:** Classic films and documentaries gain traction

### Regional Preferences
- **North America:** Comedy leads with 30% market share
- **Europe:** Drama content dominates with 35%
- **Asia:** Action and romance genres most popular
- **Latin America:** Telenovelas and series format preferred
